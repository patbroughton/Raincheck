About Raincheck

This web application is meant to serve as a quick & simple way to check weekly rainfall data in your area. There are plenty of sites where you can get more detailed information, data for other locations, etc. but Raincheck keeps the experience simple and fun to use! It's especially great for gardeners!

How it Works

When the website is loaded, your location is detected (with your permission of course). Location data is used to query the National Weather Service for nearby weather stations. The first weather station retrieved is used as the source of rainfall data. Once retrieved, the last 7 days of data is plotted in inches.
